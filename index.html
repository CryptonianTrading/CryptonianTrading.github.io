BACKGROUND & OPPORTUNITY

Times are changing. Our world is drifting away from manual trading, and professionals are beginning to employ machines to increase efficiency. Novice traders are outbehaved, outperformed, and outwitted.

Automated trading in the US stock exchanges accounted for over 75% of all shares traded in 2014, up from just 50% in 2013. This is the general trend of the equity stock, bond and securities markets, but the world of Cryptocurrencies is still young and remains relatively untapped. As it stands, there is no simple way to test algorithms on the crypto market. Algo trading, being the future of the cryptocurrency market, presents a huge opportunity - to create a platform for all traders, students or professionals, to develop, backtest, and execute their algorithms in real-time.
A big gap in the current FinTech industry is the one between financial knowledge and coding ability. This not only applies to students, but also many retail traders. We have a situation where individuals with interest and knowledge on finance doesn’t know how to code, preventing them from joining quantitative finance. If we were to find a way to bridge this gap, we can make high-level trading more accessible.

Amongst the current rise of Machine Learning, we have found a solution. Specifically, Natural language processing has its perfect application here. Our solution converts a language that an individual with a background in finance can understand, into code that can be implemented. We strive to be at the forefront of algorithmic trading on the crypto market.
